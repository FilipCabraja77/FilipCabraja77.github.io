About the center

The Foka Novalja Diving Center is located in the Straško Camp, not far from the Novalja town center.

The undersea area at the diving centre is interesting, adorned with a meadow of Neptune grass, numerous yellow and brown sponges and coastal fish, the most attractive of which are seahorses. The "Seahorse Story" project, aimed at protecting seahorses with the help of divers, began in this center. Read more about the Seahorse Story project at our website.

Diving locations

The Foka Novalja diving center is an ideal place for diver training thanks to its good location, favorable conditions, and easy access from the beach.

We are happy to work with new divers, and provide a sound basis for their subsequent diving activities in the undersea world. From this center you can go to the Tegulae and Triget diving sites. They are accessible from the coast at Stara Novalja, a 10-minute drive from the town center.
