NAUI PRO Program

Assistant Course Instructor

As an Assistant Instructor, you are certified to teach all aspects of skin and scuba diving under the supervision of a NAUI Instructor.

Apply for the program Send inquiry

About the program

The NAUI Assistant Instructor rating is a NAUI leadership-level certification.

The program introduces students to diving instruction basics and tests them in the fundamental water skills required for capable assistants. Certification authorizes you to assist active-status NAUI Instructors in dive training.

This is the best possible way to develop your instructional skills. The rating can be a step toward full Instructor, or an end in itself - match your time, talent and interests with the NAUI programs near you.

Path to the rating

  1. 1

    Entry requirements

    You hold NAUI Master Scuba Diver and NAUI Scuba Rescue Diver (or an equivalent), with at least 20 logged open-water scuba dives.

  2. 2

    Instruction basics

    You are introduced to diving instruction basics and tested in the fundamental water skills required for capable assistants.

  3. 3

    Assisting instructors

    Certification authorizes you to assist active-status NAUI Instructors, teaching all aspects of skin and scuba diving under their supervision.

Frequently asked questions

You must hold NAUI Master Scuba Diver and NAUI Scuba Rescue Diver (or an equivalent) and have at least 20 logged open-water scuba dives, varying in environment, depth and activities.

It is a NAUI leadership-level certification that introduces you to diving instruction basics and tests the fundamental water skills of a capable assistant.

You are certified to teach all aspects of skin and scuba diving under the supervision of a NAUI Instructor, and to assist active-status NAUI Instructors in dive training.
