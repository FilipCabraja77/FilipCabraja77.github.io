About us

Let's get acquainted

The Foka Diving Center has been around since 2002. Over the years we have explored the waters off the island of Pag, and in 2006 we decided to move the center to its present location, the Šimuni Camping Village. We did so to offer a higher number of attractive diving locations with abundant marine life, only a few nautical miles away from the current location in the camp. The variety of locations offers something for everyone, from locations suitable for beginners, to more demanding locations for advanced divers.
