AVELO

How it works

Avelo is a revolutionary diving system that changes the way we dive. We are proud to be the first in Croatia to bring this technology to our divers. By replacing heavy, traditional equipment with lighter and smarter design, Avelo makes every dive easier, safer, and more enjoyable.

At the heart of the Avelo system is the Hydrotank, a revolutionary system that uses water instead of air to control buoyancy. This innovation eliminates the need to constantly adjust the buoyancy compensator and thus allows for a more natural diving experience.

Experience with Avelo

Diving with Avelo is the closest thing to the feeling of lightness that freedivers have. Neutral buoyancy seems unreal, and the possibility of enjoying diving increases exponentially.

How to get started - RAD course

Sign up for the so-called RAD (Recreational Avelo Diver) course, which consists of two parts: an online course (in English) and sea trainings (two dives in one day). Part of the training is also dive analysis with the help of Shearwater Avelo mode software that helps optimize the use of Avelo equipment.
