Program mentor

Vedran Dorušić

NAUI Course Director #33934

Vedran Dorušić is a NAUI Course Director (#33934) and Instructor Trainer, and the owner of Foka Diving Center. As Course Director he leads the NAUI PRO programs at Foka: Skin Diving Instructor, Assistant Course Instructor, Divemaster and Instructor Training. He has created or co-created many projects and is an underwater archaeology enthusiast. He has been training divers and instructors on the island of Pag for more than 20 years.

From diver to instructor

Four NAUI PRO leadership programs, ordered by rank. The training is led by Vedran Dorušić, NAUI Course Director, who trains future instructors.

  1. 01

    Skin Diving Instructor

    RankA NAUI leadership-level certification in skin (breath-hold) diving.

    Want to begin working as a professional - organizing your own courses, granting certifications and conducting open-water skin dives?

    View the program
  2. 02

    Assistant Course Instructor

    RankA NAUI leadership-level certification; a step toward full Instructor.

    As an Assistant Instructor, you are certified to teach all aspects of skin and scuba diving under the supervision of a NAUI Instructor.

    View the program
  3. 03

    Divemaster

    RankThe highest NAUI leadership rating below Instructor.

    Lead dives and dive trips and take certified divers to the world's best dive sites.

    View the program
  4. 04

    Instructor Training

    RankA NAUI Instructor can teach Skin Diving Instructor, Assistant Instructor and Divemaster courses.

    Train as a NAUI Instructor, qualified to teach Skin Diving Instructor, Assistant Instructor and Divemaster courses.

    View the program
  5. 05

    Price on request
