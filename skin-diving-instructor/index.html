Apply for the program Send inquiry

About the program

This is a leadership-level certification course that trains and qualifies you to grant certifications in skin (free / breath-hold) diving.

NAUI Skin Diving Instructor training lets you join NAUI as a leadership member and independently conduct skin diving training. With the growing popularity of snorkelling and skin diving, this can bring substantial financial rewards.

You can also team-teach with a NAUI Instructor, offering the skin diving portion of the Scuba Diver Course. The course makes NAUI Leaders who can effectively teach people to enjoy skin diving at minimum risk - for individuals, schools, camps or similar groups.

Program mentor

Vedran Dorušić

NAUI Course Director #33934

Vedran Dorušić is a NAUI Course Director (#33934) and Instructor Trainer, and the owner of Foka Diving Center. As Course Director he leads the NAUI PRO programs at Foka: Skin Diving Instructor, Assistant Course Instructor, Divemaster and Instructor Training. He has created or co-created many projects and is an underwater archaeology enthusiast. He has been training divers and instructors on the island of Pag for more than 20 years.
