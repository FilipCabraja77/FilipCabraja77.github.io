About the center

Over the years we have explored the waters off the island of Pag, and in 2006 we decided to move the center to its present location, the Šimuni Camping Village.

We did so to offer a higher number of attractive diving locations with abundant marine life, only a few nautical miles away from the current location in the camp. The variety of locations offers something for everyone, from locations suitable for beginners, to more demanding locations for advanced divers.

Diving locations

In the camp itself, we have an ideal location for diving from the coast, as well as night diving.

The maximum depth is 33 meters, and since fishing is forbidden in the camp, there is always plenty of fish, unafraid of the divers. Another noteworthy location is the plateau near the island of Maun. There is a beautiful wall by the island, with red sea fans and crevices full of fish. There are 10 diving locations, each offering something special. The wall can be divided into eastern and northern parts, each one unique. The length of the wall is 1,200 amazing meters, with numerous bends and inlets. The wall also features several caves and semi-caves which are open to all diving categories. We have photographed a red coral (Coralium rubrum) at this location for SCUBAlife magazine photo shoot.
