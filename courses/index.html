NAUI courses on Pag for every level, from your very first dive to Master.

01 · Course

Skin Diver

Snorkelling and freediving for the whole family, from age 8. No prior experience is needed - your NAUI instructor coaches you through the skills and two open-water dives.

  • From age 8
  • 2 open-water dives
  • No experience needed
You earn NAUI Skin Diver certification

02 · Course

Open Water Scuba Diver

NAUI entry-level scuba certification with the fundamental skills to safely dive in open water. The course includes 6 dives to a maximum depth of 18 m, from age 10.

  • From age 10
  • 6 dives
  • Max depth 18 m
You earn NAUI Scuba Diver certification
Learn more Book
03

03 · Course

Advanced Scuba Diver

The perfect course for experienced divers without formal certification. Pass the NAUI exam and complete open-water training to earn an internationally respected NAUI card. From age 15 with proof of at least 25 dives.

  • From age 15
  • 25+ logged dives
You earn NAUI Scuba Diver certification
Learn more Book
04

04 · Course

Master Diver

The top recreational NAUI certification. Gain in-depth knowledge with at least eight open-water dives plus a choice of elective dives. From age 15 with a NAUI Advanced certification.

  • From age 15
  • 8+ open-water dives
  • NAUI Advanced required
You earn NAUI Master Scuba Diver certification
Learn more Book
05

05 · Course

Kids

Discovery dives for children from age 8 - a first, safe taste of the underwater world to a maximum depth of 5 m, always in the instructor's safe hands. Children over 10 can take the Junior open water course.

  • From age 8
  • Max depth 5 m
  • About 20 min
