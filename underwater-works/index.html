Underwater works

Underwater works and inspections

We carry out these works for private boat owners, marinas, campsites, tourist facilities, local authorities and other clients.

Request a quote Contact us

What we do

Mooring systems and berths

  • Making and installing mooring blocks - "corpo morto"
  • Installing, inspecting and securing mooring systems
  • Inspection and check of existing mooring blocks, chains, ropes and connecting elements
  • Installing and replacing chains, ropes, shackles and buoys
  • Checking the condition of berths before the start of the tourist season

Vessels, shore and infrastructure

  • Inspection of the underwater parts of a vessel
  • Recording and documenting damage to the vessel's hull
  • Inspection of propellers, rudders, shafts and intakes
  • Removing ropes, nets and other objects from the propeller
  • Inspection of pontoons, marinas, piers and port infrastructure
  • Inspection of underwater parts of the shore, foundations and structures
  • Inspection of underwater pipelines and outfalls

Documentation and special works

  • Underwater photo and video recording
  • Archaeological surveys and documentation of underwater sites
  • Biological surveys and monitoring of the seabed
  • Recording the condition of the sea floor
  • Producing photo and video reports after the inspection
  • Marking underwater installations and structures
  • Minor underwater assembly and repair works
  • Recovery of smaller objects and equipment from the sea
