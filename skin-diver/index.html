About the course

Skin Diver

Imagine floating atop a crystal-clear sea watching fish, crabs and corals - face-to-face with everything from dolphins to pilchards.

If this sounds like something you would enjoy, then the NAUI Skin Diver or Junior Skin Diver course is for you! Once you have completed your training and two open-water dives, you will have the skills you need to be a comfortable and safe Skin Diver. Your NAUI Instructor will coach you through snorkelling and freediving, and your enjoyment will grow as your skills increase.

Step by step

Course content
